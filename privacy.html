Dicestorm — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Dicestorm is a dice-rolling app for tabletop games. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Data we collect

None. Dicestorm does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We have no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

Data stored on your device

Your settings (theme, roll speed, sound, orientation, label and tablet preferences) and the current dice on the table are saved locally on your device using standard browser/app storage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it.

Network access

The app runs fully offline. It makes no network requests, contains no ads, and has no in-app purchases.

Children

Dicestorm contains no ads, no data collection, and no user-generated content, and is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page.
